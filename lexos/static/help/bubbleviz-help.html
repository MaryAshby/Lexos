The BubbleViz Tool

BubbleViz offers an alternative to word clouds as a method of visualizing the Document-Term Matrix. They present terms arranged inside circles ("bubbles") sized according to the terms' frequency within the text. BubbleViz graphs enable you to get a sense of the content in your corpus, and they are very good for presentations.

Usage

The BubbleViz tool will automatically create a graph with all the active documents. To use less or more of the documents in your corpus, visit the Manage tool to activate and deactivate documents.

Run your mouse cursor over the bubble to open a tooltip showing the number of times the term occurs in your selected document(s).

If you then wish to download the graph as a .png file, click the Save as PNG button.

Options

  • Graph Size

    • The Lexos BubbleViz tool allows you to control the Graph Size (in pixels). The default setting is 800 pixels.

  • Maxiumum Number of Terms

    • The default number of terms in the BubbleViz chart is 100, but you can also control this aspect of the graph.

  • Minimum Term Length

    • By default, Lexos does not exclude any terms from the BubbleViz graph. But you can set the minimum number of characters required in a term for it to be added to the graph.

Once you have chosen your options, click the Generate button to generate the graph.
